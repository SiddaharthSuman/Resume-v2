OBJECTIVE

The objective needs to be concise, incorporating these key points:
  • Which course and specialization do you wish to pursue? eg. To pursue MS in Computer Science with a specialization in AI/ML.
  • The specific skill sets and areas of interest that you wish to upgrade and explore through this program.
  • What do you wish to do immediately after MS? That is the target field/domain and role/s you want to work in.
  • Examples of Objectives for MS:
    1. Award-winning Senior Analyst with 2 years of professional experience expertise in Java, C#, Web Designing, and Networking aiming to pursue an MS in Data Science with a concentration in data mining and statistical analysis to later work as a Data Engineer.
    2. To pursue graduate studies in Biomedical Engineering with specialization in biomechanics to further work on devising solutions for unmet clinical needs.
    3. Quality Analyst with 5+ years of experience in Test Engineering and QC Cards seeking to pursue an MS in Computer Science to develop advanced skills in programming to later work as a Developer.
